# LANDSCAPE — Co-inhabiting
**Wildlife-corridor landscape project · Candasnos, Barranco de Valcuerna, Los Monegros (Aragón)**
Reference species: Iberian lynx (as lens/client). School of Architecture La Salle 2025–2026.

Reactivating the dry Barranco de Valcuerna as a **xeroriparian ecological spine** — a fauna-driven, arid-honest corridor connecting Natura 2000 sites, reconnecting the Hondo de la Unilla lagoon, and combining conservation with regenerative agriculture and low-touch ecotourism.
